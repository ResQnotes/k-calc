The Clinical Logic

Potassium levels shift based on pH to maintain electrochemical neutrality. This tool accounts for these transcellular shifts:

  • Acidosis (pH < 7.4): K+ exits cells. We subtract 0.7 for every 0.1 pH decrease to find the true baseline.
  • Alkalosis (pH > 7.4): K+ enters cells. We add 0.3 for every 0.1 pH increase.

Note: Always treat the patient, not just the number. Use this for clinical guidance, not as a replacement for ECG findings.
